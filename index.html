Cost Information

All prices are estimated in South African Rands (ZAR). OpEx(Operational Expenditure) represents monthly recurring costs. Annual OpEx commitment includes a 10% Volume Discount. CapEx (Capital Expenditure) represents one-time upfront costs for purchasing assets that will benefit your business for years (you own assets). Conversions are based on standard 8-hour days and 4-week months.
